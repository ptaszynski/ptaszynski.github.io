Worthwhile places on the web, plus a few personal notes.

Communities & affiliations

Computational Linguistics Group on LinkedIn — I founded a group on LinkedIn dedicated to Computational Linguistics and related fields (NLP, Corpus Linguistics, etc.) to help people interested in the topic share and obtain information about language processing.

Japonica Creativa — a student scientific circle at the Institute of Japanese Studies, Department of Oriental Studies, Adam Mickiewicz University, Poznań. I was one of the founders and served as president for several years. The circle disseminates Japanese culture and provides Japan-related information in the Poznań area.

Non-scientific writings

Hokkaido — Japan without geishas and samurais — I co-authored this book in 2005 with ten other writers. The book is a collection of chapters, each by a different author, on phenomena related to Hokkaido, the northern island of Japan. My chapter is about the night life and dark side of Sapporo, Hokkaido’s largest city. (Site: hokkaido.com.pl)
